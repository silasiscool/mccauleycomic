Hello friends! Welcome to the McCauley Daily Comic website. New daily strips will be posted at 6 A.M. daily, with a full-page special being released every Sunday. What does an intelligent young man who has no desire to go to college do after graduating from high school? RoB McCauley has been recently recruited by Duffy’s Odd Jobs, where he’ll perform a variety of hilarious tasks across many industries. This daily cartoon is intended to spread the message that who you are as a person matters more than any role you could ever chase. RoB reminds us to stay confident when others may look down on you, and to always treat others with love. On December 1st, 2025, the first McCauley strip was drawn during a study break. Since then, the strip has welcomed a cast of seven mainline characters that won’t fail to entertain you! The art style continuously evolves with every strip.
